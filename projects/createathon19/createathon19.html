2019 AT&T Create-a-thon

May 17, 2019 - May 19, 2019  |  Film

Since we made a successful short film before, we were excited and wanted to do another one. We gathered some more friends and went to the 2019 AT&T Create-a-thon in May.

We wanted to create an experience that was modern, funny, and relatable. We did some brainstorming and decided to do a quirky take on the 21st century dating scene. In our film, Garth is a skilled but awkward social media hacker. In his attempts to connect with his dates, he stalks them online but always scares them away with his research, that is until he meets his match. Please enjoy our film: L0v3.R.
